The Grain
"Teff has nourished Ethiopia for 3,000 years. Now it's baking into something new."

Teff (Eragrostis tef) is a tiny but mighty grain native to the Horn of Africa. It forms the foundation of injera — Ethiopia's beloved flatbread — and has been a dietary staple for millennia. Packed with iron, calcium, fiber, and resistant starch, it is entirely and naturally gluten-free. We source our teff directly from Ethiopian and domestic farms, honoring the heritage of this extraordinary grain in every cookie we bake.

Why Teff

The grain that gives more.

100% Gluten-Free

Teff contains no gluten — naturally. No substitutes, no blends, no compromises. Safe for celiac and gluten-sensitive diets.

Iron-Rich Superfood

Teff is one of the highest grain sources of iron, supporting energy and blood health with every serving.

High in Fiber

Resistant starch and dietary fiber support gut health and keep you fuller, longer — even in cookie form.

Sustainably Grown

Teff thrives with minimal water and no pesticides. It's one of the most climate-resilient crops on earth.
